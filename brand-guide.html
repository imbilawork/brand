01 · Brand Origin

The Honey Badger. Fearless by nature.

The name "Imbila" comes from isiZulu. Imbila.AI has adopted the honey badger as its brand animal — the most fearless creature on the planet. Pound for pound, it punches far above its weight. Tenacious, resourceful, relentless, and famously unbothered by larger predators.

This is the brand ethos: help the underdogs compete. Don't back down. Move fast. Get results.

The Zulu proverb "Imbila yeswela umsila ngokuyalezela" — the creature that lost its tail by sending others — remains the philosophical core: don't delegate your own AI journey.

10 · Agent Build Guide

For AI agents building on-brand.

Any AI agent (Claude or third-party) creating content, pages, emails, or assets for Imbila must follow these rules. This section is the machine-readable contract.

Visual Rules

  • Always import DM Sans from Google Fonts. No other typefaces permitted.
  • Use the design tokens in Section 03 as your :root block — copy them verbatim.
  • Primary background is #0A0A0A. Cards use #1F1E1E. Borders use #2A2A2A.
  • Accent colour is always Honey Gold #EDAF42 — for CTAs, links, highlights, and tags.
  • Never use gradients, glows, shadows on the logo, or colours outside this palette.
  • Border radius: 16px for cards, 10px for inputs, 100px for pills/tags/buttons.
  • Body text is #BBBBBB (grey-300). Headings are #FAFAFA (white).
  • Use the SVG honey badger mark from this guide. No raster fallbacks in new builds.

Voice Rules

  • Write as a sharp, experienced colleague — not a corporate brochure.
  • Be direct. No fluff, no hedging, no "leverage synergies."
  • Match tone to tier: measured for Enterprise, energetic for Studio, conversational for Dojo.
  • Use signature phrases: "Weeks, not months" · "Clarity into execution" · "No transformation theater"
  • Never say: "digital transformation," "cutting-edge," "paradigm shift," or "we believe that potentially..."
  • Keep the South African voice natural — not performative, not Silicon Valley.

Structural Rules for Microsites

  • Every page must include the sticky nav with: Imbila mark (SVG) + wordmark, navigation links, and version tag.
  • Use the .page wrapper at max-width 1120px with 40px padding.
  • Section labels use the pattern: 01 · Section Name — 10px, 700 weight, 0.2em tracking, uppercase, honey-dark colour.
  • H2 headings pair plain text with a .accent span in honey gold.
  • Cards: grey-900 background, grey-800 border, 16px radius, 28px padding. Hover lifts 2px and lightens border.
  • All CTAs link back to imbila.ai properties. External links get the ↗ suffix.
  • Footer: centred, minimal — logo, document title, date, confidentiality note.
